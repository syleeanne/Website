Publications

Robots in Diverse Contexts: Effects of Robots Tasks on Expected Personality

HRI 2018

The purpose of this study is to examine people's expectations and preferences for a robot's personality based on the tasks the robot is performing. Four categories of tasks were expected to be completed by robots: social, office, service, and physical. Depending on the tasks, the personalities of extraversion, conscientiousness, and openness showed significantly different values.

    • Immersive VR for numerical engagement

    VRST 2017

    This study aimed to offer audiences opportunities to have an immersive experience with the statistical figures in the news. Three different conditions for representing numerical information were implemented: 1) text, 2) infographic, 3) VR. This research proposes the new method to achieve the purpose of the journalism by transforming numbers into life-sized materials.
