My research interest is to examine the interactions between humans and diverse media such as social robots, computers, conversational agents, and interfaces using linguisitcs and design factors. Expressing the internal states of machines and interfaces have been my focused research interest. I am also interested in the moral issues of the Artificial Intelligence.

    Immersive VR for numerical engagement

  • Link

    • The aim of the study was to offer audiences opportunities to have an immersive experience with the statistical figures in the news. The study aimed to go beyond the current numerical information representation method to develop a new system for improving the numerical experience. Three different conditions for representing numerical information were implemented: 1) text, 2) infographic, 3) VR.

    Projects

    [Ongoing 2019] Expressing the interface's personalities with color and motions

    The project aims to figure out the cues to express the personality of interfaces. Visual cues (color and motion) and verbal cues (voice pitch, emotionality, gender, speed, wordiness) were selected for modalities.

    [Ongoing 2018] Designing the social dialogue model for chatbot

    Using the automation server and Google API system, users type in the Google form and the conversation data is being accumulated in the context of counseling. The accumulated data will be analyzed and tagged with social cues, such as trustworthiness, intimacy, and self-disclosure using Python Markovify modeling.
