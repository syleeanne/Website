[2018] UX research in the context of multi-agents, multi-users
(Funded by LG Electronics)

The role of Hub conversational agent (as a Broker, Mediator, and Broadcaster) was examined depending on the tasks the agent perform. The perceived preferences of multiple users were analyzed depending on different personas of AI speakers. The UX research was conducted with desk research, lab experience, and interview. This project was funded by LG Electronics.
