[2017] The effect of Height in VR setting

This project aimed to explore the impact of the height of the opponent avatar on the in immersive virtual environment. For this study, we developed a multiplayer virtual environment with lunar surface and bodily immersed astronaut avatars and conducted a preliminary user study manipulating the opponent avatar's height. The perception of height and emotional response when encountering the opponent avatar were observed in particular. Our results imply that height perception and subsequent emotional responses in physical reality are appropriately reproduced in virtual reality. Considering the extended application of VR in various fields, the study result provides meaningful insights for avatar design in social VR development.
